Handyman Services

Oscar has the tools and expertise for almost any odd job around your house. All job are fixed price, and they are based on a rate of $30/man-hour + materials. Oscar can do everything from small finishing work, to larger jobs that require a crew of workers.

  • painting
  • tile, wood, and concrete floors
  • carpentry/woodworking
  • stone walls, wood fences, and other barriers
  • repairs
    • water damage
    • rusty/creaky hinges, gates, and other metal objects
    • broken windows
    • malformed doors
  • hauling/disposal
